Meet the Owner

Founder & Owner

Mubbashar Hameed

Hameed Photo Studio — Since 1982

Mubbashar Hameed has led Hameed Studio for years, growing it from a single photo shop into a trusted name across Gujranwala for personalized mugs, frames, and gifts. Alongside the studio, he also runs a group of appointment-booking services that help people across Pakistan complete the paperwork needed for overseas work — from medical fitness tests to skill verification and biometric appointments.
